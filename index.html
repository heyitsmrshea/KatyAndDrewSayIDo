Wedding day is Beach Formal. Think lightweight suits, linen, flowy dresses, and polished beach-friendly footwear.

Not at all. Join what fits your trip and skip what doesn’t. Use the RSVP section for the optional events that need a headcount.

What should we pack?

Bring resort wear, beach essentials, comfortable shoes for excursions, and one polished wedding-day look. June is warm, bright, and breezy.
