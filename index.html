Frequently Asked

What is the dress code?

The wedding itself is Resort Elegant. For gentlemen, linen suits or dress pants with a button-down. For ladies, midi or maxi dresses in light, airy fabrics. Please avoid shorts and flip-flops for the ceremony.

Are all events required?

Not at all. Some events are optional and designed to give everyone time to relax. Use the RSVP section to choose the events that make sense for your trip.

What should we pack?

Bring resort wear, beach essentials, comfortable shoes for excursions, and one polished wedding-day look. June is warm, bright, and breezy.

What about transportation?

All events listed on the itinerary are held on-resort or include round-trip transportation from the resort lobby. No rental car is necessary.
